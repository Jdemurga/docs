---
title: "Get Started with Vexel Horizon"
description: "Open Vexel Horizon, link your Sovereign session, explore your dashboard, run a what-if simulation, and vote on the Community Board in a few minutes."
---

Vexel Horizon is ready to use the moment you open it in a browser. In Guest Mode you have immediate access to live ecosystem statistics, the Market Hub, and the global sentiment ticker. Link your Vexel Mobile identity via Sovereign Link and the terminal switches to your personal vault, unlocking AI Proposals, the Intelligence Lab, and the Community Board. This guide walks you through the flow from first load to your first signed action.

<Note>
  Guests can browse the Dashboard (Guest Mode) and the Market Hub without authenticating. Sovereign Link is required for the Intelligence Lab, the Community Board, and approving any AI Proposal.
</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Vexel Horizon">
    Navigate to your Vexel Horizon URL in a browser. The terminal loads in **Guest Mode** by default. The dashboard displays:

    - **Global Ecosystem Value** — total value across all Sovereign nodes on The Mesh
    - **Collective Velocity** — the aggregate yield velocity of the ecosystem, in dollars per second
    - **Market Intelligence chart** — the Vexel Ecosystem Index
    - **Market ticker** — BTC, ETH, AAPL, PAXG scrolling in the header

    The system status bar confirms the connection: **SYSTEM ONLINE | Mesh Latency: 14ms**. Pro cards (Neural Allocation, AI Recommendation, AI Proposals) are covered by a frosted-glass blur until you authenticate.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authenticate with Sovereign Link">
    Click **Authorize Guardian** in the header, or **Authorize Pro Features** in the sidebar. A QR code appears along with your unique session ID — for example, `[SESSIONID: 4f2c-9a11]`.

    Open Vexel Mobile, go to **Profile > Sovereign Link**, and scan the QR. The browser receives a `sessionLinked` event over the WebSocket, removes every blur, and reloads the dashboard with your personal vault data.

    See [Authenticate with Sovereign Link](/sovereign-link) for the full guide.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore your Dashboard">
    Once in Sovereign Mode, the Dashboard shows your personal data:

    - **[Your Name] Vault Value** — total market value of your assets, interpolated every 100 ms between Mesh pulses
    - **Personal Velocity** — your per-second growth rate
    - **Neural Allocation** — a doughnut chart showing your holdings by asset class
    - **AI Recommendation** — the neural network's top conviction pick with short reasoning
    - **AI Proposals** — actionable rebalances and emerging-opportunity cards
    - **Market Intelligence chart** — your personal portfolio performance line
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse the Market Hub">
    Click **Market Hub** (🌐) in the sidebar. A responsive grid of trending cards appears, each showing symbol, type, price, 24h change, and **Neural Confidence** percentage. The grid is fetched live from `GET /api/market/trending` and the header ticker continues updating on every Mesh pulse.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run a what-if simulation">
    Click **Intelligence Lab** (💡) in the sidebar. The Lab opens with the **Crypto Hyper-adoption** scenario active. Pick a scenario from the sidebar — *Systemic Credit Crunch*, *Inflation Spike*, or the default — and the viewport renders a projected impact chart plus an AI analysis explaining the drivers. Click **Sync Strategy to Mobile** to push the recommendation to Vexel Mobile for biometric approval.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Vote on a proposal">
    Click **Community Board** (🗳️). Review the active proposals — each card includes a category badge, description, and Neural Consensus insight. Click **Support** or **Against** to open the Sovereign Guardian modal, then scan the QR from Vexel Mobile to sign the vote.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Horizon does not execute trades directly. Every write action — AI Proposal approval, simulation sync, governance vote — is signed on Vexel Mobile using your biometric credentials. Keep your mobile device at the ready for the fastest authorization flow.
</Tip>
