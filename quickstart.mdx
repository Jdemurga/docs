---
title: "Get Started with Vexel Horizon"
description: "Learn how to open Vexel Horizon, authenticate with Sovereign Link, explore your portfolio dashboard, and execute your first trade in under 5 minutes."
---

Vexel Horizon is ready to use the moment you open it in a browser. In Collective Mode you have immediate access to live global ecosystem statistics, trending assets, and real-world asset opportunities. Add your Sovereign Link authentication and the terminal upgrades to your personal vault, enabling trading, governance, and automated strategy deployment. This guide walks you through the complete flow from first load to your first executed swap.

<Note>
  Guest users can explore the Dashboard (in Collective Mode), Market Hub, and RWA Nexus without authenticating. Sovereign Link is only required for write operations.
</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Vexel Horizon">
    Navigate to the Vexel Horizon URL in your browser. The terminal loads in **Collective Mode** by default. The dashboard displays global ecosystem statistics:

    - **Global Ecosystem Value** — the total value of all assets across the Vexel network, updating in real time
    - **Collective Velocity** — the aggregate yield velocity of the entire ecosystem, expressed as dollars per second
    - **Trending assets** — assets with unusual activity detected by the AI, shown in the signals feed
    - **AI Confidence** — the neural network's current confidence score for overall market stability

    The system status bar at the top confirms the connection: **SYSTEM ONLINE | Mesh Latency: 14ms**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authenticate with Sovereign Link">
    To access your personal vault and enable write operations, you need to link your Vexel Mobile identity.

    Click **Connect Sovereign** in the top-right header. A QR code appears in the Sovereign Guardian modal along with a unique session ID. Open Vexel Mobile, go to **Profile > Sovereign Link**, and scan the QR code.

    The browser auto-updates to Sovereign Mode — the dashboard title changes to your personal vault, and the sidebar user area shows your name and a pulsing green indicator confirming an active session.

    See [Authenticate with Sovereign Link](/sovereign-link) for the full step-by-step guide.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore your Dashboard">
    Once in Sovereign Mode, the Dashboard shows your personal financial data:

    - **Vault Value** — the total market value of all your assets in USD, recalculated every 100 milliseconds using yield velocity interpolation between Mesh pulses
    - **Personal Velocity** — how much your capital is growing per second based on your active positions
    - **AI Confidence** — the neural network's confidence score, displayed as a percentage (for example, `96.5%`)
    - **Portfolio Performance chart** — an interactive line chart of your personal portfolio history, with 1D, 1W, 1M, and ALL time range controls

    The **AI Proactive Signals** feed on the right shows live buy and sell recommendations generated by the neural network, each with an Approve button to initiate the corresponding action.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse the Market Hub">
    Click **Market Hub** in the left sidebar to open the asset terminal. The Market Hub displays a live table of assets including Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), Apple (AAPL), and Pax Gold (PAXG), each with:

    - Current price
    - 24-hour price change
    - AI Confidence score

    Use the search bar at the top to filter assets by symbol — type `BTC`, `AAPL`, `Gold`, or any supported ticker and the table filters instantly. The trending grid above the table highlights assets with unusual activity detected by the AI, including their confidence score and price change direction.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Execute your first swap">
    Click **Trading** in the left sidebar. The Sovereign Execution Engine section opens.

    1. In the **Sell** panel, select the asset you want to sell from the dropdown (USD, BTC, or ETH) and enter the amount in the number field.
    2. In the **Buy** panel, select the asset you want to receive (ETH, BTC, or PAXG).
    3. Click **EXECUTE ORDER**.

    The status badge updates to **Scanning | AI Guardrails Active** and the feedback panel appears with the message "Starting AI Security Scan...". The AI checks market liquidity, smart contract security, and price direction before proceeding.

    If the scan passes, the status moves to **Pending | Waiting for Mobile Sign** and the feedback displays your AI safety score and verdict. Open Vexel Mobile to sign the transaction. Once signed, the terminal receives a confirmation and displays the finalized swap details including the audit ID.

    <Warning>
      If the AI scan returns an **Aborted | Security Risk** status, the order has been blocked due to detected risk factors. Review the details shown in the feedback panel before retrying.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  After your first swap, explore the **Neural Strategist** to automate future trades. Drag AI Sentiment, Yield Scan, Threshold Check, and Auto-Swap nodes onto the canvas to build a strategy, then deploy it to the Vexel Mesh — no code required.
</Tip>
