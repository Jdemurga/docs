The Sovereign Guide is the built-in knowledge base rendered inside Vexel Horizon itself. It lives under the **Guide** sidebar entry (`#guideSection`) and gives you a reading-friendly reference without leaving the terminal. Every article is localized through `lang/en.json` and `lang/es.json`, and the content switches instantly when you change the active language.

## How the Guide is organized

The Guide is split into two categories that match how Sovereigns operate the terminal:

| Category | Topics |
|---|---|
| **Manual de uso** | Dashboard (The Vault), Market Hub (The Pulse), Gobernanza (The Mesh), Trading (The Engine), Strategist (The Brain) |
| **Base técnica** | Yield Velocity, AI Confidence, Lattice Security, Sovereign Reasoning, The Mesh, Tutorial: Estrategia |

Selecting a topic in the left sidebar swaps the content of `#guideTopicContent` with the localized article. Articles are stored in `app.js` as `knowledgeBaseEs` and `knowledgeBaseEn` objects keyed by topic id, and the active language is read from `localStorage.vexel_lang`.

## Localization

- The Guide respects the language stored in `localStorage.vexel_lang` (`es` or `en`).
- Article titles and categories are localized through `data-i18n` attributes in `index.html`.
- Bodies live in `knowledgeBaseEs` / `knowledgeBaseEn` in `app.js`, so contributors can extend the Guide by adding new topic keys to both objects.

## Implementation notes

- The Guide shell is defined in `index.html` as `#guideSection` with a two-pane layout: a `.guide-sidebar` list of topics and a `.guide-viewport` that renders the active article.
- Topic selection is wired via a delegated click listener on `.guide-nav-item` elements; the handler reads `dataset.topic` and calls `updateGuideContent(topic)`.
- Navigating to the Guide does not require a Sovereign session — it is available in Guest Mode as a reference while you set up your Sovereign Link.
