## Getting started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Vexel Horizon">
    Navigate to the Vexel Horizon terminal in your browser. You land in **Guest Mode**, showing global ecosystem stats — no login required. Pro surfaces are blurred until you authenticate.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect via Sovereign Link">
    Click **Authorize Guardian** in the header. Open the Vexel Mobile app, go to **Profile > Sovereign Link**, and scan the QR code to bind your biometric identity to this session.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore your dashboard">
    Once linked, the dashboard switches to **Sovereign Mode** — showing your personal vault value, Neural Allocation doughnut, live AI Proposals, and your personal portfolio performance.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Act on an insight">
    Approve an AI Proposal, run a scenario in the Intelligence Lab, or cast a Community Board vote. Every write action pushes a biometric prompt to Vexel Mobile for signing — the terminal itself never commits capital.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Sovereign Link authentication is required for the Intelligence Lab, the Community Board, and approving any AI Proposal. The Dashboard (Guest Mode) and the Market Hub are accessible without logging in.
</Note>
