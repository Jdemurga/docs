## Getting started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Vexel Horizon">
    Navigate to the Vexel Horizon terminal in your browser. You'll land in **Collective Mode**, showing global ecosystem stats — no login required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect via Sovereign Link">
    Click **Connect Sovereign** in the top-right corner. Open the Vexel Mobile app, go to **Profile > Sovereign Link**, and scan the QR code to authenticate with your biometric identity.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore your dashboard">
    Once linked, your dashboard switches to **Sovereign Mode** — showing your personal vault value, live yield velocity, and personalized AI signals.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Execute your first trade">
    Navigate to **Trading**, select an asset pair, enter an amount, and click **Execute Order**. The AI security scanner runs automatically before any swap is confirmed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Sovereign Link authentication is required for trading, governance voting, and deploying neural strategies. The Dashboard and Market Hub are accessible without logging in.
</Note>
