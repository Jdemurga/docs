The Neural Strategist includes an AI generation input that translates plain-English trading instructions directly into canvas node flows. Instead of dragging and sequencing blocks manually, you describe what you want the strategy to do — and the system places the corresponding nodes for you in approximately 1.2 seconds.

## Where to find the AI input

The AI generation bar sits at the top of the canvas workspace, anchored to the centre of the screen. It is marked with a **✨** icon. You can type your instruction into the text field and submit it with the **GENERATE** button or by pressing **Enter**.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Type your intent in plain English">
    Click the AI input bar at the top of the canvas and enter a plain-English description of your trading logic. For example:

    ```text
    Sell BTC if AI sentiment is very negative
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit the command">
    Click **GENERATE** or press **Enter**. The Sovereign Reasoning feed immediately confirms receipt:

    ```
    [AI] Processing order: "sell BTC if AI sentiment is very negative"
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for the node layout to appear">
    After approximately 1.2 seconds, the AI clears the current canvas and places the generated nodes. The feed updates with:

    ```
    Strategy synthesized based on your intent.
    ```

    The input bar clears automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the generated flow">
    Inspect the nodes placed on the canvas. Add blocks from the left sidebar or right-click nodes to remove them if the layout needs adjustment.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deploy to the Mesh">
    Click **Deploy to Mesh** to activate the strategy on the decentralized network. Deployment requires an active Sovereign Link session.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Logic mapping

The AI maps keywords in your command to specific node combinations. Understanding this mapping helps you write more precise instructions.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Buy / Swap commands">
    Commands containing **"buy"** or **"swap"** generate:

    | Block | Position |
    |---|---|
    | AI Sentiment | First |
    | Auto-Swap | Second |

    **Example commands:**
    - `Buy ETH when market sentiment is positive`
    - `Swap to gold when yield drops`
    - `Buy BTC on positive sentiment`
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Sell / Sentiment commands">
    Commands containing **"sell"** or **"sentiment"** generate:

    | Block | Position |
    |---|---|
    | AI Sentiment | First |
    | Threshold Check | Second |
    | Guardian Alert | Third |

    **Example commands:**
    - `Sell BTC if sentiment turns negative`
    - `Sell BTC if AI sentiment is very negative`
    - `Monitor sentiment and alert me`
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Other commands">
    Commands that contain neither of the above keywords generate:

    | Block | Position |
    |---|---|
    | Yield Scan | First (default starting point) |

    **Example commands:**
    - `Protect my portfolio`
    - `Watch for yield changes`
    - `Monitor performance`
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Note>
  AI generation builds the visual layout on your canvas — it does not deploy or execute the strategy. Review the generated nodes, adjust the flow as needed, and then click **Deploy to Mesh** when you are ready to activate it.
</Note>


```text

```

## After generation

Once the AI places nodes on the canvas, you are in full control of the resulting flow. You can:

- Drag additional blocks from the **Neural Blocks** sidebar to extend the logic
- Right-click any node to remove it
- Click **Clear Canvas** to start over
- Click **Deploy to Mesh** to activate the strategy (requires Sovereign Link)

<Tip>
  Click the **📖 VIEW GUIDE** button at the bottom of the Strategist sidebar to open the in-app Tutorial on strategy building. It walks through block placement and deployment step by step.
</Tip>

<Warning>
  Generating a strategy from text replaces whatever is currently on the canvas. If you have an existing node layout you want to keep, note it down or deploy it before running a new AI generation command.
</Warning>
