Neural Strategy Templates are pre-built strategy configurations you can load onto the canvas with a single click. Instead of placing and sequencing blocks manually, you start from a working node flow and customise it to match your requirements. Vexel Horizon ships with two built-in templates: **Smart Stop-Loss** and **Autonomous DCA**.

## Available templates

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Smart Stop-Loss" icon="shield">
    Monitors market sentiment and triggers a guardian alert notification when sentiment turns very negative. The alert surfaces on Vexel Mobile, where you approve or reject the protective action.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Autonomous DCA" icon="chart-line">
    Watches your yield performance and automatically triggers a dollar-cost averaging swap when your returns drop below target — without requiring manual approval for each cycle.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Template node flows

Each template loads a specific sequence of Neural Blocks. The order of blocks on the canvas defines the execution logic.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Smart Stop-Loss">
    **Blocks loaded:** AI Sentiment → Threshold Check → Guardian Alert

    | Position | Block | Role |
    |---|---|---|
    | 1 | AI Sentiment | Reads global news tone as the trigger |
    | 2 | Threshold Check | Evaluates whether sentiment has crossed a negative threshold |
    | 3 | Guardian Alert | Sends a notification to Vexel Mobile for your approval |

    The strategy does not execute a swap automatically. Instead, the Guardian Alert block notifies you on Vexel Mobile, where you decide whether to approve the protective action.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Autonomous DCA">
    **Blocks loaded:** Yield Scan → Auto-Swap

    | Position | Block | Role |
    |---|---|---|
    | 1 | Yield Scan | Monitors returns and triggers when yield falls below target |
    | 2 | Auto-Swap | Executes the dollar-cost averaging swap automatically |

    This template runs without a manual approval step. When your yield drops below the configured threshold, the Auto-Swap fires immediately on the Vexel Mesh.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Using a template

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Neural Strategist">
    Click **Strategist** in the sidebar. The canvas workspace opens. Sovereign Link authentication is required — Vexel Horizon redirects you to authenticate if you are not already signed in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a template">
    In the **Neural Templates** section of the left sidebar, click the template you want to load:
    - **🛡️ Smart Stop-Loss** for sentiment-based guardian alerts
    - **📈 Autonomous DCA** for yield-triggered dollar-cost averaging
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the loaded node flow">
    The canvas clears any existing nodes and places the template's pre-configured block sequence. Inspect the nodes and their connections to confirm the flow matches your intent.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customise the flow (optional)">
    Add additional Neural Blocks from the left sidebar by dragging them onto the canvas, or right-click any existing node to remove it. You can extend a template without restriction.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deploy to the Mesh">
    Click **Deploy to Mesh** to activate the strategy. Vexel Horizon runs a neural simulation of your graph and presents a verification report. Deployment requires an active Sovereign Link session — the network uses your biometric signature to authorise capital movements.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Templates are starting points, not fixed configurations. Load a template, then drag in extra blocks to extend the logic — for example, add a **Threshold Check** before the **Auto-Swap** in the Autonomous DCA template to introduce a numeric guardrail.
</Tip>

<Note>
  Loading a template replaces everything currently on the canvas. If you have unsaved work you want to keep, note down your node arrangement before clicking a template button.
</Note>

## What happens when you load a template

When you click a template button, the canvas clears, the template's nodes are placed at predefined positions, and the connections between them are drawn automatically. The Sovereign Reasoning feed confirms the load with a status message such as:

```
Template 'Smart Stop-Loss' loaded. AI nodes deployed.
```

or

```
Template 'Autonomous DCA' loaded. Sequential flow ready.
```

You can then proceed to customise or deploy directly from that state.
