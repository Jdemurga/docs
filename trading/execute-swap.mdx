The Sovereign Execution Engine is Vexel's trading interface, built for maximum security and minimum slippage. Every swap passes through an automated AI security scan before any capital moves. If the scan clears the trade, the order is parked and routed to your Vexel Mobile app for biometric authorization — so you retain final control at every step.

## Prerequisites

You must have an active **Sovereign Link** session before you can execute a swap. Accessing Trading without authentication redirects you to the Sovereign Link screen automatically.

<Info>
  To establish a Sovereign Link session, open the Vexel Mobile app, navigate to **Profile > Sovereign Link**, and scan the QR code displayed in the terminal. See the [Sovereign Link](/sovereign-link) page for full setup instructions.
</Info>

## Execute a swap

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Trading">
    Click **Trading** in the left sidebar. The Sovereign Execution Engine panel opens, and the status badge reads **Idle | Ready for Ops**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the asset to sell">
    Open the **Sell** dropdown on the left side of the swap panel. Choose the asset you want to sell:
    - Dollar (USD)
    - Bitcoin (BTC)
    - Ethereum (ETH)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the amount">
    Type the amount you want to sell into the input field below the Sell dropdown.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the asset to buy">
    Open the **Buy** dropdown on the right side of the swap panel. Choose your target asset:
    - Ethereum (ETH)
    - Bitcoin (BTC)
    - Physical Gold (PAXG)

    The **Est** field below the dropdown updates to show the estimated receive amount based on current market pricing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit the order">
    Click **EXECUTE ORDER**. The AI security scan starts immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for the AI security scan">
    The status badge changes to **Scanning | AI Guardrails Active** and the execution feedback panel appears below the swap form. The scan checks:

    - Market liquidity — to avoid losses from insufficient order book depth
    - Smart contract security — of the destination vault
    - Near-term price probability — whether conditions are expected to improve

    The scan runs automatically. You do not need to take any action during this step.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the outcome">
    The scan produces one of two results:

    **Aborted — trade blocked**

    The status badge changes to **Aborted | Security Risk** and the feedback panel shows:

    ```
    DANGEROUS: [reason]
    ```

    The swap is cancelled. No funds move. Review the stated reason (examples: low liquidity, smart contract risk) before attempting the trade again.

    **PendingAuthorization — ready to sign**

    The status badge changes to **Pending | Waiting for Mobile Sign** and the feedback panel shows:

    ```
    AI Score: [score]. Secure payload parked. Check Vexel Mobile to sign.
    ```

    The swap payload is held securely. Proceed to the next step.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize on Vexel Mobile">
    Open **Vexel Mobile** on your device. A pending authorization request appears. Use your biometric signature (Face ID or fingerprint) to approve the transaction.

    Once you sign, the terminal receives the confirmation via Socket.io and the status updates to:

    ```
    Finalized | Audit ID: [id]
    ```
  </Step>
</Steps>

## After authorization

When the swap finalizes, the execution feedback panel shows the AI reasoning behind the trade. Quantum-secured transactions display a **LATTICE_VERIFIED** badge and the panel border highlights in blue. The Sovereign Reasoning Feed logs the completed swap:

```
Swap finalized: [qty] [symbol]. Audit: [id]
```

The audit ID is a permanent on-chain reference you can use to verify the transaction independently. The feedback panel clears after five seconds and the status returns to **Idle | Ready for Ops**.

<Warning>
  Never share your session ID with anyone. Each swap requires a fresh biometric signature from your registered mobile device. Vexel staff will never ask for your session ID.
</Warning>

<Note>
  If the connection to the execution engine drops during a swap, the status shows **Error | Connection Lost**. No funds move when a connection error occurs. Refresh the page and retry the swap once the connection is restored.
</Note>
