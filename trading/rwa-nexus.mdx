RWA Nexus is the bridge between physical assets and digital liquidity. **RWA** stands for Real World Assets — tangible instruments like gold bars, real estate, and institutional debt that Vexel tokenizes and makes available for fractional investment on-chain. Every listing is legally backed, held by an audited custodian, and comes with a linked institutional audit report you can review before committing any capital.

## How tokenization works

Vexel converts ownership of a physical asset into a digital token. Each token represents a legally recognized claim on the underlying asset, which is held in custody by an independent audited institution. When you invest, you purchase a fraction of that token — not the asset itself — which means you can access high-value instruments without buying them outright.

This model has two key properties:

- **Legal backing** — every token is tied to documentation that establishes the ownership chain and custodian relationship.
- **Fractional access** — you invest in a digital fraction, removing the capital barrier that normally limits access to assets like commercial real estate or gold bullion.

## What each listing shows

Each RWA card in the Nexus displays:

- **Asset name** — the name of the underlying instrument
- **Verification status** — the current audit and compliance state of the listing
- **Category** — the underlying asset class (e.g., gold, real estate, institutional debt)
- **APY** — the annualized yield percentage for the position
- **Issuer** — the institution responsible for the token issuance
- **Institutional Audit Report** — a link to the legal documentation backing the asset

## Invest in an RWA

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open RWA Nexus">
    Click **RWA Nexus** in the left sidebar. The page loads the current inventory of verified listings fetched live from the platform:

    ```bash
    GET /api/market/public/rwa
    ```

    You can browse all listings without authenticating.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the listings">
    Read through the available assets. Check the APY, issuer name, and verification status for each listing that interests you.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Read the audit report">
    Click **✦ Institutional Audit Report** on any listing to open the legal documentation in a new tab. Review the custodian details, asset composition, and compliance status before proceeding.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start the investment">
    Click **Invest Now** on your chosen listing. The **Sovereign Guardian** modal appears, displaying a QR code specific to this investment action.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize on Vexel Mobile">
    Open **Vexel Mobile** and scan the QR code shown in the modal. Use your biometric signature to authorize the investment. Once signed, the modal closes and the position is recorded.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Data freshness

RWA listings are publicly accessible without a session — anyone can browse and review audit documentation. Investing requires an active Sovereign Link session. The listing data is fetched each time you open the RWA Nexus section, so APY values and verification statuses reflect the current state.

<Tip>
  RWA positions contribute to your personal Yield Velocity. Assets with higher APYs increase the per-second earnings figure shown on your dashboard. Check the yield of each listing carefully before investing.
</Tip>

<Note>
  Sovereign Link authentication is required to invest. If you click **Invest Now** without an active session, the Sovereign Guardian modal still appears — but the investment will not process until your biometric signature is provided from a linked mobile device.
</Note>
