The Market Hub is your institutional data terminal. It pulls live pricing, order flow, and AI analysis from multiple sources — Binance, CoinGecko, and Bloomberg — and surfaces everything in a single interface. Browse assets, read AI confidence scores, spot trending opportunities, and jump straight into a trade from one place.

## Open the Market Hub

Click **Market Hub** in the left sidebar. The section loads immediately with no authentication required.

## What you see

The Market Hub has two main areas.

### RWA Nexus hero banner

At the top of the page, a full-width banner reads **RWA NEXUS (TOKENIZATION)** with the subtitle "Real World Assets on the Blockchain." This banner links the Market Hub to the broader RWA Nexus feature. Click **RWA Nexus** in the sidebar to explore tokenized real-world asset listings directly.

### Asset table

Below the banner, the asset table lists the instruments Vexel monitors. Each row contains five columns:

| Column | Description |
|---|---|
| **ASSET** | Name and ticker symbol |
| **PRICE** | Current market price in USD |
| **24H CHANGE** | Price movement over the past 24 hours |
| **AI CONFIDENCE** | Neural network confidence score for the asset (see below) |
| **ACTION** | Trade button that opens the Trading section for that asset |

Click **Trade** on any row to go directly to the Trading section and execute a swap for that asset.

## Search assets

Use the search bar at the top of the Market Hub to filter the asset table in real time. Type a symbol or name — `BTC`, `AAPL`, `Gold` — and the table narrows to matching rows instantly. Clear the field to restore all results.

## Trending assets

Below the asset table, the **Trending Assets** section shows instruments that display unusual activity or AI-detected opportunities. This data is fetched live from the platform:

```bash
GET /api/market/trending
```

Each trending card shows the asset symbol, type, current price, 24-hour change direction, and the neural confidence score. The system refreshes this list automatically as new signals arrive.

## AI confidence score

Every asset carries an **AI Confidence** percentage. This score is generated by the neural network from three inputs:

- **News sentiment** — analysis of recent coverage and market commentary
- **Order volume** — depth and direction of current order book activity
- **Historical volatility** — the asset's price stability over comparable periods

A higher score indicates stronger agreement across these signals. The score is not a price prediction — it reflects how clearly the model can characterize the current market state for that asset.

<Tip>
  Assets with an AI Confidence above 90% have the strongest neural network consensus. PAXG (99.8%) and BTC (98.2%) currently lead the table.
</Tip>
