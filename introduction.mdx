Vexel Horizon is the web hub of the Vexel ecosystem — a professional financial terminal that puts institutional-grade intelligence directly in your browser. It aggregates live market data, runs AI-driven trading signals, surfaces real-world asset opportunities, and routes every write operation through your biometric identity on Vexel Mobile. Whether you are monitoring global ecosystem health as a guest or managing your personal vault as an authenticated sovereign, Vexel Horizon adapts to your session state in real time.

## Two operating modes

Vexel Horizon runs in one of two modes depending on whether you have linked a Vexel Mobile session.

**Collective Mode** is the default for unauthenticated visitors. The dashboard shows global ecosystem statistics — total ecosystem value, collective yield velocity, trending assets, and the AI confidence score for the broader Vexel network. You can browse the Market Hub and RWA Nexus freely in this mode.

**Sovereign Mode** activates automatically once you authenticate via Sovereign Link. The terminal switches to your personal vault: your net worth replaces the global ecosystem value, yield velocity reflects your individual portfolio, and write operations such as trading, governance voting, and neural strategy deployment become available.

## Sovereign Link requirement

Read-only sections — Dashboard (in Collective Mode), Market Hub, and RWA Nexus — are accessible without authentication. Any operation that writes to the Vexel Mesh requires a linked Sovereign session: trading, governance voting, neural strategy deployment, and risk insurance activation all prompt the Sovereign Guardian modal and wait for a cryptographic signature from your Vexel Mobile app.

See [Authenticate with Sovereign Link](/sovereign-link) to set up your session before proceeding.
