Vexel Horizon is the professional-grade web terminal of the Vexel ecosystem — a high-density, read-focused dashboard designed to extend Vexel Mobile. Guests see global Mesh metrics and a blurred preview of Pro features; Sovereign users unlock personal vault data, AI proposals, what-if simulations, and DAO voting. By design, the terminal does not execute trades directly: every write action is handed off to your Vexel Mobile device for biometric approval.

## Two operating modes

**Guest Mode** is the default state on load. Local storage is empty, so Vexel Horizon shows public ecosystem metrics — total ecosystem value, collective yield velocity, trending assets, and Mesh status. Pro surfaces (AI Proposals, Neural Allocation, Intelligence Lab, Community Board) are covered by a frosted glass blur until you authenticate.

**Sovereign Mode** activates automatically when the `sessionLinked` WebSocket event confirms a successful scan of the Sovereign Link QR from Vexel Mobile. The `body` receives the `is-sovereign` class, which removes every blur and swaps ecosystem data for your personal vault — net worth, neural allocation, AI proposals, and portfolio performance.

## Read-heavy by design

Horizon is fundamentally a read-heavy client. It displays deep insights, but it does not execute trades directly. Clicking **Approve** on any AI Proposal, **Sync Strategy to Mobile** in the Intelligence Lab, or **Support / Against** on a proposal triggers a Sovereign Guardian WebSocket request that pushes a biometric prompt to your Vexel Mobile device. The mobile signature is the only thing that can commit capital — the terminal alone cannot.

## Sovereign Link requirement

The Dashboard (in Guest Mode) and the Market Hub are accessible without authentication. The Intelligence Lab and Community Board are protected sections: attempting to navigate to them without a session redirects automatically to the Sovereign Link QR screen. Approving any AI Proposal also requires an active Sovereign session.
