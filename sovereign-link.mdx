title: "Authenticate with Sovereign Link"
description: "Sovereign Link is Vexel Horizon's QR authentication flow. Scan a QR with Vexel Mobile to bind your biometric identity to the browser and unlock Pro features."
---

Sovereign Link is the QR-based authentication system that ties your Vexel Mobile biometric identity to a live browser session on Vexel Horizon. There are no passwords. The terminal generates a unique session ID, encodes it into a QR code, and waits for your mobile app to scan and sign it. Once the link is established, Horizon removes the frosted-glass blur from every Pro surface and loads your personal vault data.

## What Sovereign Link unlocks

Without a Sovereign session, Pro features in the dashboard (Neural Allocation, AI Recommendation, AI Proposals) stay blurred. The following sections additionally redirect to the authentication screen if you try to open them unauthenticated:

- **Intelligence Lab** — what-if simulations against your Sovereign portfolio
- **Community Board** — DAO voting on active proposals
- **AI Chat and Voice** — typed or spoken commands to the Sovereign AI

The Dashboard (in Guest Mode) and the Market Hub remain available to every visitor.

<Info>
  You only need Sovereign Link when you want to act on the data rather than observe it, or when you want to see your personal vault instead of ecosystem metrics.
</Info>

## How to authenticate

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Sovereign Link prompt">
    Click **Authorize Guardian** in the top-right of the header, or **Authorize Pro Features** (🔒) in the sidebar. Either action opens the Sovereign Guardian modal.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive your session QR code">
    The terminal generates a QR code that encodes your unique session ID. The ID is displayed beneath the QR — for example, `[SESSIONID: 4f2c-9a11]`. This ID is cryptographically unique to your current browser tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Vexel Mobile">
    On your phone, open Vexel Mobile and navigate to **Profile > Sovereign Link**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scan the QR code">
    Use Vexel Mobile to scan the QR displayed in your browser. The app reads the session ID and launches the biometric authorization flow.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Session activates in the browser">
    After a successful scan, the browser receives a `sessionLinked` event over the Socket.io connection. The terminal stores your JWT and user profile, adds `is-sovereign` to the `body` element (which removes every blur), dismisses the modal, and reloads the dashboard with your personal vault data — vault value, personal velocity, neural allocation, and portfolio performance.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Session storage

On successful authentication, Vexel Horizon stores your session credentials in the browser's `localStorage` under `vexel_jwt` (the token) and `vexel_user` (your profile). The terminal reads these on every page load to determine whether to start in Guest Mode or Sovereign Mode. Your credentials are tied to the session ID established during the QR handshake and are never transmitted in plain text.

<Warning>
  Your JWT remains in the browser until you explicitly disconnect or clear local storage. Do not authenticate on shared or public devices unless you intend to disconnect before leaving.
</Warning>

## Disconnecting your session

To end your Sovereign session, click **✕ DISCONNECT SESSION** in the user area at the bottom of the sidebar. This clears `vexel_jwt` and `vexel_user`, removes the `is-sovereign` class, and reloads the terminal in Guest Mode.

A remote revocation is also possible: if your session is invalidated from Vexel Mobile, the terminal receives a `session_revoked` event, logs it to the feed (`CRITICAL: Session revoked from mobile device.`), and force-reloads into Guest Mode after a brief delay.

## Protected section redirects

If you navigate directly to the **Intelligence Lab** or **Community Board** without an active session, the terminal automatically redirects you to the Sovereign Link authentication screen. Complete the QR scan and the terminal returns you to the section you requested.

<Tip>
  Keep the Sovereign Guardian modal open on desktop and Vexel Mobile ready on your phone for the fastest authentication flow. Each QR is tightly scoped to one session and one action — it cannot be replayed.
</Tip>
