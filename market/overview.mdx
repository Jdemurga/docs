The Market Hub is your global data terminal inside Vexel Horizon. It combines a live price ticker with a neural-powered trending grid, so you can spot opportunities across crypto, equities, and commodities from one screen. The page is read-only and available to every visitor — no Sovereign Link session is required to browse.

## Open the Market Hub

Click **Market Hub** (🌐) in the left sidebar. The section loads the trending grid immediately and the main header ticker continues to stream updates in the background.

## Live market ticker

The top header of the terminal scrolls a real-time ticker with the most-watched instruments — BTC, ETH, AAPL, and PAXG by default. Each ticker item shows the current price, the 24-hour change, and an up or down arrow colored green for gains or red for losses. The ticker is powered by the `marketPulse` Socket.io event and refreshes on every Mesh pulse (approximately every 15 seconds).

## Trending grid

The Market Hub body is a responsive grid of **trending asset cards**, fetched from the platform each time you open the section:

```bash
GET /api/market/trending
```

Each card shows:

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| **Symbol** | The ticker (for example, `BTC`, `ETH`, `AAPL`) |
| **Type** | The asset class — `CRYPTO`, `EQUITY`, `COMMODITY` |
| **Price** | Current market price in USD |
| **24h change** | Price movement with a colored arrow (▲ green for gains, ▼ red for losses) |
| **Neural Confidence** | The AI confidence score for the trend signal, shown as a percentage |

Cards are rendered in a CSS grid with a minimum column width of 250 pixels and auto-fill columns, so the layout adapts to your browser width.

## AI Confidence on trending cards

The **Neural Confidence** percentage on each card reflects how strongly Vexel's neural models agree on the trend signal for that asset. The value is derived from three parallel inputs:

- News sentiment
- Order flow depth and direction
- Historical volatility

Higher scores mean tighter agreement across models. See [AI Confidence](/concepts/ai-confidence) for details on how the score is calculated.

<Tip>
  Check the Neural Confidence score before acting on a trending signal. A reading above 90% indicates strong consensus across the neural models.
</Tip>

## What the Market Hub does not include

The Market Hub is a discovery surface, not an execution surface. There is no Buy, Sell, or Invest button on the Market Hub itself — those actions live inside the Vexel Mobile app and are authorized through the Sovereign Guardian signing flow. Horizon's role is to surface intelligence; Vexel Mobile executes it.

<Note>
  If you want to act on a trending signal, open Vexel Mobile and initiate the trade from there. Any resulting signature request can still be authorized against your active browser Sovereign session.
</Note>

## Data freshness

- **Ticker** — updates on every Mesh pulse (~15 seconds) via `marketPulse`
- **Trending grid** — fetched each time you open the Market Hub section

If the connection to Vexel Core drops, the terminal falls back to Fail-Safe Sandbox Mode. Cached data remains visible, but live updates pause until the Mesh connection is restored.
