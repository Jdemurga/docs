Vexel's AI does not operate as a black box. **Sovereign Reasoning** is the transparency layer that surfaces the AI's decision rationale directly in the interface — every signal, every alert, and every recommendation comes with a plain-language explanation of what triggered it and why. You always know what the AI is doing and the logic behind it.

## Where to see Sovereign Reasoning

You encounter Sovereign Reasoning in three places:

- **AI Proposals card** on the Dashboard — personalized, actionable recommendations with human-readable rationale attached to each one
- **Activity feed** (`aiFeed` / `aiSignalsFeed`) — a live stream of Mesh events, intent confirmations, security notices, and ledger records
- **Intelligence Lab analysis block** — the plain-language reasoning that accompanies each what-if simulation result

## Feed entry types

Each entry in the Sovereign Reasoning feed is prefixed with a label that tells you the category of the signal at a glance.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="[BUY SIGNAL] / [SELL SIGNAL]">
    Trade recommendations with the AI's explicit rationale. For example: "BTC is undervalued by 2.4% according to sentiment analysis." These entries give you the specific factors that led the AI to flag an opportunity.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="[STRATEGY]">
    Rebalancing suggestions for your existing strategies. The AI monitors your current allocations and surfaces adjustments when conditions shift.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="[INTENT]">
    A confirmation of what the AI understood from your voice or chat command. When you speak a command or type in the AI Chat, the terminal logs what the AI interpreted so you can verify it heard you correctly before any action is taken.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="[AUTONOMOUS]">
    Signals generated by an approved automated strategy running on The Mesh. These entries notify you that an autonomous action is pending or has been proposed, giving you the opportunity to authorize or reject it via the Sovereign Guardian flow.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="[MACRO] / [LIQUIDITY]">
    Alerts triggered by macroeconomic factors or liquidity conditions — for example, a significant shift in broad market sentiment or a detected drop in order-book depth for an asset you hold.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="CORE">
    Network-level information from The Mesh — such as the number of active Sovereign Nodes or confirmation that the Mesh is active and processing.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="SECURITY">
    Authorization and session events. Examples include session establishment on Sovereign Link, external authorization requests, and session revocation notices.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="LEDGER">
    Completed trade audit records written after a swap finalizes. Each entry includes the asset symbol, quantity, and an audit ID — the same audit ID shown in the execution feedback panel — creating a permanent, readable record of every executed action.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Guest Mode vs. Sovereign Mode

The feed adapts based on your authentication state:

- **Guest Mode** (no active Sovereign session) — the feed shows ecosystem-level signals: network activity, trending asset detections, and Mesh status entries. Pro-only surfaces (AI Proposals, Neural Allocation) stay blurred.
- **Sovereign Mode** (Sovereign session active) — the feed shows your personal signals: trade recommendations, intent confirmations, autonomous strategy notifications, and your personal LEDGER entries.

<Info>
  Your Dashboard header and yield labels also shift between modes. In Guest Mode, the Dashboard shows "Global Ecosystem Value" and "Collective Velocity." In Sovereign Mode, these update to your personal vault value and personal yield velocity.
</Info>

## Asking the AI to explain a signal

<Tip>
  If a feed entry is unclear, use the in-app AI Chat to ask for an explanation in plain language. Click **"Talk to Vexel"** in the bottom-right corner of the terminal or tap the microphone button in the Chat overlay. You can type or speak your question — for example, "Why did you flag a sell signal on ETH?" — and the AI will respond in the chat and log an `[INTENT]` entry to the feed confirming what it understood.
</Tip>

<Note>
  The AI Chat and voice command features require an active Sovereign session. If you are in Collective Mode, you will be directed to authenticate via Sovereign Link before commands can be processed.
</Note>
