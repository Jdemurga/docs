---
title: "How to Vote on Governance Proposals"
description: "Cast your vote on Vexel DAO proposals using biometric signature authorization from Vexel Mobile — ensuring only you control your governance participation."
---

Voting on a governance proposal requires two things: an active Sovereign Link session and your Vexel Mobile app. When you cast a vote, the terminal opens the Sovereign Guardian modal and generates a unique QR code. You scan that code with Vexel Mobile to sign your vote biometrically. This design ensures that only the rightful account holder can participate in governance — not anyone who might have access to your terminal session.

## Prerequisites

- An active Sovereign Link session. If you are not authenticated, the terminal redirects you to the Sovereign Link screen when you navigate to Governance.
- Vexel Mobile installed on your device and linked to your Sovereign account.

## Voting steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Governance">
    Click **Governance** in the sidebar. The terminal loads all active proposals from the network.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse the active proposals">
    Read each proposal card carefully. Review the proposal title and description to understand what change is being proposed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the Neural Consensus insight">
    Each card includes a **Neural Consensus** block with an AI-generated analysis of the proposal's theoretical impact on the ecosystem. Read this before deciding your position.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check the meshSentiment badge">
    Review the meshSentiment badge on the card to understand the community's current leaning. This data is pulled live from The Mesh.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Cast your vote">
    Click **Authorize FOR** to vote in favor of the proposal, or **Authorize AGAINST** to vote against it. The terminal opens the Sovereign Guardian modal immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scan the QR code with Vexel Mobile">
    The Sovereign Guardian modal displays a QR code and the action title — for example, "Voting FOR: [Proposal Title]". Open Vexel Mobile and scan the QR code to sign your vote with your biometric identity.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Vote submitted">
    After a successful biometric scan, your vote is submitted to The Mesh and propagated across the decentralized network.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Why votes require a mobile signature

Votes require a cryptographic signature from Vexel Mobile to guarantee that only you — the rightful account holder — can cast a vote on your behalf. This prevents vote manipulation, impersonation, and unauthorized participation even if someone else has access to your terminal session.

<Warning>
  Each vote generates a unique session QR code that expires after use. Never share your QR code while a voting action is in progress.
</Warning>

## Neural Consensus and your vote

The Neural Consensus insight on each proposal card summarizes the AI's predicted impact on yield, liquidity, and network stability. It is a read-only analysis — the AI does not vote, weight votes, or override the outcome. You retain full decision-making authority over your governance participation.

<Tip>
  Check the Neural Consensus insight on each proposal before voting. It summarizes the predicted impact on yield, liquidity, and network stability, and can help you make a more informed decision.
</Tip>
