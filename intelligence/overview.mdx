The Intelligence Lab is Vexel Horizon's simulation environment — a Pro feature that lets you model the impact of macroeconomic events on your Sovereign portfolio before they happen. Pick a scenario, and the Mesh runs your current holdings through the selected event, returning a projected impact chart alongside a plain-language AI analysis. The Lab replaces the earlier node-based Neural Strategist with a focused, scenario-driven workflow.

## Accessing the Intelligence Lab

Click **Intelligence Lab** (💡) in the left sidebar.

<Warning>
  The Intelligence Lab is a protected Pro section. If you do not have an active Sovereign Link session, the terminal redirects you to the authentication screen automatically. See [Authenticate with Sovereign Link](/sovereign-link) to link your session.
</Warning>

## What the Lab shows you

The Intelligence Lab is split into two panels.

### AI Scenarios sidebar

The left column lists the predefined scenarios you can simulate. Clicking a scenario marks it active and triggers the simulation against your current portfolio.

| Scenario | Icon | Projected impact |
|---|---|---|
| Crypto Hyper-adoption | 🚀 | High Growth |
| Systemic Credit Crunch | 📉 | High Volatility |
| Inflation Spike | 💸 | RWA Resilience |

A **Run Neural Audit** button at the bottom of the scenarios panel runs a custom neural audit across your portfolio without a preset macro scenario.

### Simulation viewport

The main column displays the simulation result for the currently selected scenario.

- **Title** — the scenario name (for example, "Simulation Result: Crypto Hyper-adoption")
- **Confidence badge** — the neural confidence percentage for the simulation (for example, `94% Confidence`)
- **Chart** — a Chart.js plot showing the projected portfolio value against the baseline over the scenario horizon
- **AI Analysis** — a human-readable explanation of the drivers behind the projection
- **Sync Strategy to Mobile** — sends the recommended strategy to your linked Vexel Mobile app for biometric approval

## Running a simulation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Intelligence Lab">
    Click **Intelligence Lab** in the sidebar. The Lab opens with the default scenario (**Crypto Hyper-adoption**) already active.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a scenario">
    Click a card in the **AI Scenarios** sidebar to switch simulations. The selected card highlights, and the viewport updates with the new projection.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the chart">
    Inspect the projected impact curve. The chart compares the scenario outcome to a baseline so you can see the delta against "no event" conditions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Read the AI Analysis">
    The analysis block below the chart explains the key drivers. For example, in a Crypto Hyper-adoption scenario the AI may suggest increasing RWA allocation to hedge correlation risk.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sync the recommendation (optional)">
    Click **Sync Strategy to Mobile** to push the AI's suggested rebalance to Vexel Mobile. Approve or reject the recommendation from the app using your biometric signature — the simulation alone does not move capital.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Neural Audit

The **Run Neural Audit** button triggers a full audit of your portfolio without a macro scenario attached. Use it when you want the AI to evaluate correlation, drawdown exposure, and allocation drift across your full holdings rather than against a single macro event.

<Info>
  Simulation results are advisory. The Intelligence Lab does not execute trades or rebalances automatically — every recommended action has to be approved from Vexel Mobile. Horizon is read-heavy by design.
</Info>

## Interpreting simulation confidence

The confidence badge on each simulation follows the same conventions as the rest of Vexel Horizon:

| Score range | Interpretation |
|---|---|
| 95% and above | Very high confidence — strong consensus across neural models |
| 80% – 94% | Moderate confidence — models broadly agree |
| Below 80% | Lower certainty — treat as directional, not prescriptive |

See [AI Confidence](/concepts/ai-confidence) for the full score methodology.

<Tip>
  Run the same scenario against multiple portfolios by switching the active vault in Vexel Mobile before syncing the recommended strategy. The Lab always simulates against your currently selected Sovereign portfolio.
</Tip>
