The AI Proposals surface on the Dashboard combines actionable recommendations with a live reasoning feed. The neural network continuously analyzes market sentiment, allocation drift, price action, and your active strategies, then surfaces personalized proposals on the right of the dashboard — along with a feed of Mesh events, intent confirmations, and ledger entries.

## Where proposals appear

The Dashboard has two complementary surfaces for AI output:

- **AI Proposals for Your Success** — the main proposal card, stacked at the top of the main column. Each entry has descriptive text and action buttons (`View on Mobile`, `Approve Now`).
- **Activity feed** — a rolling list of Mesh and session events (`aiFeed` / `aiSignalsFeed`), including network status, security events, ledger entries, and autonomous signals.

## Proposal types

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Portfolio Rebalance" icon="rotate">
    Notifies you when an allocation drifts from its target. Example: *Your 'Tech 2026' portfolio has drifted. AAPL is now 35% (Target: 25%). Sell surplus to buy BTC.* Approve to push the rebalance to Vexel Mobile for biometric sign-off.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Emerging Opportunity" icon="arrow-trend-up">
    Surfaces newly listed RWA assets or trending signals that fit your risk profile. Example: *New RWA asset 'RealEstate-NYC' listed. Matches your Moderate risk profile. Suggest 5% allocation.*
  </Card>
  <Card title="Buy / Sell signal" icon="signal">
    Short-term trade recommendations from the neural network — for example, *BTC is undervalued by 2.4% according to sentiment analysis.* Includes the asset symbol, direction, and reasoning.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Strategy rebalance" icon="sliders">
    Adjustments to an existing autonomous strategy, such as a DCA plan that has drifted from target. Example: *Strategy `DCA_GOLD` suggests a rebalance. Approve buy of 0.5 PAXG?*
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Activity feed entries

The lower-density feed (`aiFeed` / `aiSignalsFeed`) is prefixed by category so you can scan it at a glance:

| Prefix | Meaning |
|---|---|
| `CORE` | Mesh-level information — active Sovereign nodes, trending detections |
| `SECURITY` | Session events and authorization requests |
| `LEDGER` | Completed swaps and vote audit IDs |
| `SYSTEM` | Connection status, Fail-Safe Sandbox Mode, pulse confirmations |
| `[INTENT]` | Confirmation of a voice or chat command the AI interpreted |
| `[AUTONOMOUS]` | Signals generated by an approved strategy running on The Mesh |
| `[MACRO]` / `[LIQUIDITY]` | Macro or liquidity alerts |
| `BRN_AI` / `Sovereign` / `Vexel` | Conversational turns surfaced from the AI Chat overlay |

## Acting on a proposal

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review the proposal">
    Read the proposal card text. Rebalance and opportunity proposals include the affected portfolio, the deviation, and the AI's suggested action. Buy / Sell signals include the asset symbol and the reasoning behind the call.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Approve or View on Mobile">
    Click **Approve Now** to authorize directly, or **View on Mobile** if you want to review the full context in Vexel Mobile before deciding. Approving calls `showOnMobile()` (for dashboard proposals) or `approveAiTrade()` (for feed trade signals).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in the Sovereign Guardian modal">
    A modal appears with a QR code and your session ID. Open **Vexel Mobile**, go to **Profile > Sovereign Link**, and scan the QR to sign with your biometric credentials. The signed action is submitted to the execution engine.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Approving a proposal requires an active Sovereign Link session. The modal still renders without a session, but the action will not process until your biometrics sign it on a linked mobile device.
</Warning>

## Guest Mode behavior

Without an active session, Pro-only surfaces — including the AI Proposals card — are blurred behind the `auth-blur` filter. The activity feed remains visible and shows ecosystem-level events (`CORE` messages, Mesh active count, trending detections) but personal proposals do not populate until you enter Sovereign Mode.

## Voice and chat

The activity feed is shared with the in-app **AI Chat overlay** and the **Talk to Vexel** voice assistant. Any command you issue — typed or spoken — is logged with the `[INTENT]` prefix, followed by the AI's response. Voice and chat both require a Sovereign session; unauthenticated commands redirect to the Sovereign Link screen.
