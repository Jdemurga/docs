The AI Proactive Signals feed is the dashboard's real-time recommendation channel. The neural network continuously analyzes market sentiment, price action, and your active strategies, then surfaces actionable signals directly in the right-side panel — no manual chart reading required. Each signal tells you what the AI recommends, why it recommends it, and gives you a single button to act on it.

## Signal types

The feed surfaces four categories of entries:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="BUY SIGNAL">
    A recommendation to open or increase a position. The signal includes the asset, the detected opportunity, and the analysis behind it — for example, a sentiment-derived undervaluation percentage.
  </Card>
  <Card title="SELL SIGNAL">
    A recommendation to close or reduce a position based on deteriorating sentiment, price thresholds, or risk factors detected by the neural network.
  </Card>
  <Card title="STRATEGY">
    A rebalancing or adjustment recommendation for one of your existing automated strategies, such as a DCA plan that has drifted from its target allocation.
  </Card>
  <Card title="CORE / SYSTEM">
    Informational messages from the Mesh: connection status, pulse confirmations, trending asset detections, and session events. These do not require action.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

CORE entries look like this:

> Vexel Mesh Active: 4,821 Sovereign Nodes linked.

> Trending detected: ETH. Confidence: 87%

## How to act on a signal

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review the signal">
    Read the signal text in the feed. BUY and SELL signals include the asset symbol and the reasoning the neural network used (e.g., sentiment analysis, undervaluation percentage). STRATEGY signals include the name of the affected strategy and the proposed action.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Approve">
    Click the **APPROVE TRADE** or **APPROVE** button on the signal card. The terminal registers the signal with the relevant asset symbol and trade direction.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in the Sovereign Guardian modal">
    A modal appears with a QR code and your session ID. Open **Vexel Mobile**, navigate to **Profile > Sovereign Link**, and scan the QR code to sign the authorization with your biometric credentials. Once signed, the trade is submitted to the execution engine.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Approving a trade requires an active Sovereign Link session. If you are not authenticated, clicking Approve opens the Guardian modal, but the trade will not be submitted until you complete mobile sign-in. Unauthenticated users cannot authorize trades.
</Warning>

## Feed behavior and ordering

New entries are prepended to the top of the feed as they arrive. The feed is populated from two sources:

- **On dashboard initialization:** Trending assets detected by the Mesh are posted as `CORE` entries, each showing the asset symbol and the neural confidence percentage.
- **On socket events:** Session events, Mesh pulse confirmations, and any action confirmations post `SECURITY`, `LEDGER`, or `SYSTEM` entries in real time.

The feed element is shared with the voice assistant. Commands you issue through the voice interface or the AI chat overlay also appear in the same feed under their respective source labels (`SYS`, `BRN_AI`, `Sovereign`).
