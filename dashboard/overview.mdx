The Dashboard is the central view of Vexel Horizon, fusing live market data with your personal or ecosystem-level financial intelligence. Every figure on screen updates in real time — no refresh, no polling delay. Whether you are browsing as a guest or operating inside an authenticated Sovereign session, the dashboard renders the same layout with data scoped to your context.

## Two operating modes

The dashboard runs in one of two states depending on whether you have an active Sovereign Link session.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Guest Mode (unauthenticated)">
    When you visit Vexel Horizon without authenticating, the dashboard runs in **Guest Mode**. It shows aggregate ecosystem data instead of personal wealth, and every Pro-only surface (Neural Allocation, AI Recommendation, AI Proposals) is covered by a frosted glass blur until you authenticate.

    - The primary card displays **Global Ecosystem Value** — total value across all Sovereign nodes on The Mesh.
    - The yield counter shows **Collective Velocity**, the ecosystem-wide capital growth rate per second.
    - The Market Intelligence chart renders the **Vexel Ecosystem Index**.

    Data is fetched from two public endpoints on load:

    ```
    GET /api/market/public/ecosystem
    GET /api/market/public/yield/status
    ```

    A `CORE` message in the feed confirms the number of active Sovereign nodes currently linked to The Mesh.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Sovereign Mode (authenticated)">
    After a successful Sovereign Link session, the dashboard switches to **Sovereign Mode**. The `body` element receives the `is-sovereign` class, which automatically removes the blur from every Pro-only card and unlocks the full layout.

    - The primary card label becomes **[Your Name] Vault Value**, populated from your stored profile.
    - The yield counter shows **Personal Velocity** — your individual capital growth rate per second.
    - The Market Intelligence chart renders your **Personal Portfolio performance**.

    Data is fetched from authenticated endpoints on load:

    ```
    GET /api/market/dashboard
    GET /api/market/yield/personal
    GET /api/market/history?symbol=BTC&type=crypto
    ```
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Dashboard layout

The Dashboard is a three-column grid: a stats column on the left, and a wide main column on the right that spans two grid cells.

### Left column — stats

<CardGroup cols={1}>
  <Card title="Total Liquidity">
    Displays the current total value in USD, formatted to two decimal places and updated every 100 ms via linear interpolation between Mesh pulses. In Sovereign Mode the header reads "[Your Name] Vault Value"; in Guest Mode it reads "Global Ecosystem Value". A **Velocity** line underneath shows either **Personal Velocity** or **Collective Velocity** in dollars per second.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Neural Allocation (Pro)">
    A Chart.js doughnut chart showing the breakdown of your Sovereign portfolio across assets — BTC, ETH, AAPL, Cash by default. The chart updates whenever the server returns an `allocation` map with the dashboard payload. Blurred in Guest Mode.
  </Card>
  <Card title="AI Recommendation (Pro)">
    A high-conviction neural pick with short reasoning — for example, *BUY BTC @ $64k — 14% deviation from 200EMA with bullish news sentiment*. Also blurred in Guest Mode.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Main column — AI Proposals and Market Intelligence

The main column stacks two cards.

**AI Proposals for Your Success** — a list of personalized, actionable proposals such as:

- **🔄 Portfolio Rebalance** — notifies you when a portfolio drifts from target allocations and offers one-click Approve / View on Mobile actions.
- **📈 Emerging Opportunity** — surfaces newly listed RWA assets or trending signals that match your risk profile.

Each proposal has two buttons: **View on Mobile** (dispatches an informational push to Vexel Mobile) and **Approve Now** (opens the Sovereign Guardian modal for biometric signing).

**Market Intelligence** — a Chart.js line graph tracking portfolio performance or the Ecosystem Index over time, with **LIVE** and **PRO MODE** toggles in the card header.

## Main header

The top header carries three elements:

- **Title + system status** — the terminal name, a **Sovereign Terminal** badge, a pulsing connection indicator, and the live `Mesh Latency` reading (for example, `Mesh Latency: 14ms`).
- **Market ticker** — a scrolling ticker of the most-watched assets (BTC, ETH, AAPL, PAXG), populated by the `marketPulse` event.
- **Authorize Guardian button** — opens the Sovereign Link authentication screen when you are not yet in a Sovereign session.

## Real-time updates via The Mesh

The dashboard subscribes to the `marketPulse` Socket.io event. The Mesh emits this event approximately every 15 seconds, delivering an updated `totalValue`, `yieldVelocity`, `globalSentiment`, and asset prices.

When a pulse arrives:

- `currentNetWorth` and `yieldVelocity` are updated immediately.
- The velocity display refreshes to the new value formatted as `+$X.XXXX/sec`.
- The market ticker repaints with the latest prices.
- The system status indicator pulses green for 500 ms, then returns to blue.

Between pulses, a 100 ms interval interpolates the net worth forward, keeping the counter smooth without waiting for the next Mesh heartbeat.

<Note>
  You do not need to interact with the dashboard to keep the data current. The chart, ticker, and cards all listen to socket events directly.
</Note>

## AI Proposals feed

The secondary feed (`aiFeed` / `aiSignalsFeed`) logs Mesh activity, intent confirmations, and security events alongside the dashboard. Each actionable signal includes an **Approve** button. Clicking it opens the Sovereign Guardian modal, which requires a mobile signature before the action is submitted.

See [AI Proposals](/dashboard/ai-signals) for a full breakdown of proposal types and how to act on them.
